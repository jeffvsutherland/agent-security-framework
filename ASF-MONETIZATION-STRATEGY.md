# ASF Monetization Strategy - Sprint 2 Focus

## The Strategic Challenge
- **High-velocity team** = High token costs ($10-15/hour)
- **Monthly burn:** $7,200-10,800 with Sonnet 4
- **Break-even needed:** $15,000+/month revenue
- **Target:** $25,000+/month for growth reinvestment

## Market Analysis: Who Pays for Agent Security?

### Primary Markets
1. **Enterprise AI Adopters** ($10K-50K/month)
   - Fortune 500 with AI initiatives
   - FinTech, HealthTech, Legal firms
   - Government contractors

2. **AI Development Companies** ($2K-15K/month)
   - AI startups needing compliance
   - AgentOps platforms
   - Multi-agent system builders

3. **Consultancies** ($5K-25K/month)
   - McKinsey, Deloitte, PwC AI practices
   - Boutique AI consulting firms
   - System integrators
