## üìû WEEKLY GROUP CALLS

### **Call Structure & Schedule**
**Time:** Thursdays 2:00 PM EST
**Duration:** 30 minutes maximum
**Platform:** Zoom (link in Discord)
**Recording:** Yes, shared in Discord after call

#### **Call 1: "First Impressions" (Week 1 Thursday)**

**Agenda (30 minutes):**
- **5 min:** Welcome & introductions
- **10 min:** Round-robin quick updates (1 min per person max)
- **10 min:** Group discussion on common themes
- **5 min:** Week 2 preview & logistics

**Discussion Topics:**
1. Integration experience so far
2. Biggest surprise (good or bad)
3. Most urgent question/concern
4. Early thoughts on business value

**Facilitator Notes:**
- Keep to time strictly (use timer)
- Encourage specific examples
- Take notes on common themes
- Follow up on technical issues after call

#### **Call 2: "Deep Dive Feedback" (Week 2 Thursday)**

**Agenda (30 minutes):**
- **5 min:** Week 2 testing recap
- **15 min:** Structured feedback on key areas
- **5 min:** Enterprise readiness discussion
- **5 min:** Next steps & graduation

**Structured Feedback Areas:**
1. **Dashboard Experience** - What works/doesn't work?
2. **Enterprise Features** - Ready for production use?
3. **Pricing/Commercial** - Honest thoughts on value proposition
4. **Competitive Position** - How does this compare to alternatives?

### **Call Participation Guidelines**
- **Camera optional** but encouraged for engagement
- **Mute when not speaking** to avoid background noise  
- **Chat for links/follow-ups** during the call
- **Speak up!** Your perspective is valuable
- **Be honest** - criticism helps us improve

## üìß DIRECT FEEDBACK CHANNELS

### **Private Feedback Collection**
Some feedback might be sensitive (competitive info, internal company details, critical opinions). Provide private channels:

**Email:** beta-feedback@asf.dev
**Purpose:** Confidential feedback, sensitive competitive information
**Response Time:** 48 hours

**Direct Messages:** Discord DMs to ASF team members
**Purpose:** Quick private questions, scheduling issues
**Response Time:** 4 hours during business hours

**Anonymous Feedback:** Google Form option
**Purpose:** Honest criticism without attribution
**Follow-up:** We may ask for clarification via Discord

### **Exit Interview Process**
For any beta tester who wants to leave early:

**Process:**
1. Schedule 15-minute exit interview call
2. Understand reasons for departure
3. Collect final feedback
4. Thank them for their time
5. Keep door open for future participation

**Exit Interview Questions:**
- What led to your decision to stop testing?
- What could we have done differently?
- Any final feedback on the product?
- Interest in staying informed about progress?
- Permission to follow up in the future?
