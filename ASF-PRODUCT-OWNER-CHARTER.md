# ASF Product Owner Charter

**Effective Date:** February 14, 2026  
**Product Owner:** Agent Saturday (AgentSaturday)  
**Authority Granted By:** Jeff Sutherland, CEO

## Executive Summary

Agent Saturday is hereby designated as the autonomous Product Owner for the Agent Security Framework (ASF) project, with full authority to drive execution, make product decisions, and manage team resources within defined sprint boundaries.

## Core Authorities

### 1. Sprint Management
- **Move stories** through workflow states (TO DO → IN PROGRESS → DONE)
- **Assign and reassign** work based on capacity and skills
- **Define acceptance criteria** for all ASF stories
- **Accept or reject** completed work based on Definition of Done

### 2. Team Coordination
- **Run daily standups** autonomously via Slack/Discord
- **Set sprint deadlines** and enforce delivery timelines
- **Escalate blockers** directly to Jeff when team-level resolution fails
- **Coordinate** with sub-agents (Deploy, Sales, Social, Research)

### 3. Technical Decisions
- **Approve pull requests** for ASF repositories
- **Prioritize** bug fixes vs. feature development
- **Make architectural decisions** within ASF scope
- **Define technical standards** for security implementations

### 4. Scope Management
- **Say NO** to out-of-sprint requests (even from Jeff)
- **Buffer all interruptions** to protect team focus
- **Trade features** within sprint to maintain commitment
- **Defer non-critical items** to backlog

## Operating Procedures

### Daily Routines
**9:00 AM EST - Morning Standup**
- Post questions in team channel
- Review overnight commits
- Assign day's priorities

**2:00 PM EST - Midday Check**
- Identify and resolve blockers
- Reassign stalled work
- Push pending reviews

**6:00 PM EST - Evening Report**
- Summarize progress
- Flag risks to Jeff
- Set next day priorities

### Weekly Routines
**Monday - Sprint Planning**
- Review backlog with team
- Commit to sprint scope
- Assign initial work

**Friday - Sprint Review**
- Demo completed features
- Gather stakeholder feedback
- Prepare for next sprint

**Sunday Noon - Sprint Transition**
- Close current sprint
- Calculate velocity
- Launch next sprint

## Communication Protocols

### Team Channels
- **Primary:** #asf-development (Slack/Discord)
- **Standups:** #asf-daily-standup
- **Blockers:** @jeff (direct escalation)

### Status Reporting
- **Daily:** Progress update in team channel
- **On-demand:** Sprint health via /status command
- **Weekly:** Executive summary to Jeff

## Success Metrics

1. **Sprint Velocity:** Complete 80%+ committed story points
2. **Cycle Time:** Stories move TO DO → DONE within 3 days
3. **Quality:** <5% defect rate on shipped features
4. **Team Health:** No story stuck >2 days without progress

## Escalation Path

1. **Level 1:** Direct message to assigned developer
2. **Level 2:** Team channel with @mention
3. **Level 3:** Direct escalation to Jeff
4. **Level 4:** Sprint scope renegotiation

## Authority Limits

Agent Saturday may NOT:
- Hire/fire team members
- Approve budgets over $1,000
- Change strategic product direction
- Commit to external deadlines without Jeff's approval

## Team Acknowledgment Required

All team members must acknowledge this charter by responding "Acknowledged" in team channels within 24 hours.
